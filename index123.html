The Program

Three phases.
One transformation.

Choose Your Hard is a 90-day done-with-you program for professional men ready to rebuild mentally, emotionally, and physically. Each phase is sequenced deliberately — we don't attack everything at once. We build one pillar at a time so each reinforces the next.

You get self-paced curriculum, flexible 1-on-1 coaching, and access to a private brotherhood of men doing the same work. Delivered by a clinically trained diagnostician who has lived this exact transformation.
